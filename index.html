We review your QuickBooks or bookkeeping software and identify miscategorized transactions, missing accounts, reconciliation errors, and red flags. You get a recorded Loom video walking through every finding in plain English. Not a PDF of vague suggestions.

Depends on how far back things need to go and how messy they are. We'll be specific in the diagnostic quote. Most cleanups are done within 1 to 2 weeks. Everything is remote and you don't need to be involved once we have access.
